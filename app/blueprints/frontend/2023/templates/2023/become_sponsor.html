Become Our Sponsor

 

FlaskCon is a conference for Flask users. Our mission is to improve the Flask community and ecosystem that you use daily. This work can’t be done without the generous financial support that organizations like yours provide.

The Flask Community Workgroup has more initiatives to further the Flask ecosystem.

An outstanding conference requires a significant budget and we look to companies in the Python and Open Source community to support this initiative.

There are dozens of reasons your company may be eager to contribute. Here are a few:

  • Recruiting new talent ranging from to intermediate-senior Python developers and engineers, Open source Maintainers and Community builders, and university to bootcamp graduates.
  • Building brand awareness and relationships in the Python and Open Source community.
  • Supporting professional development for your technical and non-technical teams.
  • Supporting the development of Python language and Open Source community, which is largely run and maintained by volunteers around the globe.
  • The conference program is usually focused on helping new speakers present new python topics, or speaking for the first time. You can also view our previously recorded talks on Youtube https://www.youtube.com/@FlaskCon
  • This helps us lay ground for the next upcoming in-person event in the US.

The Pallets is a subsidiary of the Python Software Foundation, which is a 501(c)(3) non-profit.

Most sponsors fund this sponsorship either out of their marketing budget or recruiting budget, depending on their goals for sponsorship, and all of our events have a legal tax-deductible status, so you may be eligible to receive a tax-deductible.
